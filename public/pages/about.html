Forum History

Liverpool Business Forum is a group of business owners that work together to develop relationships and leads for one another’s businesses. Collectively we are a powerful group that can lobby for more opportunities to work within the private and public sectors.

We work collaboratively within a trusted environment for the betterment of the forum and the local community. We recommend and develop business referrals based on the trust and recommendations that we deliver excellence.

Started in 2011 we have a committed membership who are looking to grow. We are based in the Baltic Triangle area of Liverpool.

We meet fortnightly at Fodder Canteen and a selection of outside and internal speakers are selected to talk on the latest business development for the benefit of forum members. We develop opportunities for new businesses for on another.
